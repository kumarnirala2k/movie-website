About Patna Cinema

About Patna Cinemas

Patna Cinemas Limited is revolutionizing the movie-going experience in India as the nation’s first and fastest-growing chain of Smart Luxury Cinemas. With a presence spanning over 300 screens, 30+ operational franchises, and many more in development, Patna is setting the benchmark for accessible premium entertainment. Since its inception in 2009, the company has been deeply rooted in the world of movies, specializing in film marketing, promotions, advertising, in-film branding, distribution, and rights management across Gujarati, Bollywood, and other regional languages. As pioneers in Smart Cinemas, Patna offers three distinct models—Express, Signature, and Luxuriance—that cater to a wide range of audiences while blending affordability with cutting-edge technology. This innovative approach ensures an unparalleled cinematic experience, combining comfort, convenience, and luxury.

Why Patna Cinemas?

Patna Cinemas is more than just a movie destination—it’s a promise of quality and innovation. With a mission to provide the most comfortable and enjoyable cinematic experience, Patna is dedicated to offering attractive pricing without compromising on luxury or technology. For Franchise Owners, Patna Cinemas deliver exceptional value with its high-return business model and industry-leading occupancy rates of over 60%. Whether you’re an audience member or a business partner, Patna Cinemas represents a future-forward approach to cinema, where entertainment meets excellence.
